Design Continuity Note
Phase 2 adds tabs to the existing Phase 1 panel — no layout changes. The map, topbar, and panel shell remain identical. What changes: the Score, Zoning, and Comps tabs activate with content. A new "Firm History" section appears within the Score tab. The "Brief" button in the action bar now fires the full-screen brief overlay. Violet (#a78bfa) is the Phase 2 accent — distinct from Phase 1's teal so users intuitively understand "this is AI-generated."
